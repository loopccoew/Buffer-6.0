🏦 Level 0: Introduction to Banking

What is a Bank?

A bank is a safe place where people can:

  • 💰 Keep money (called deposits)
  • 💸 Take loans (borrow money)
  • 🔁 Transfer money to others
  • 📈 Earn interest on savings

How Does a Bank Work?

People deposit money (e.g. ₹1,000). Bank uses that to give loans (e.g. ₹10,000 for a car). The borrower pays back with interest. Bank earns money and shares a portion with savers as interest.

What Can You Do With a Bank Account?

💰 Save your money safely
🏧 Withdraw cash using ATM or bank branch
📤 Send money to family or friends
🛒 Pay bills or shop online
📝 Apply for loans and credit cards

Types of Bank Accounts

Type Purpose
Savings Account For saving money and earning interest
Current Account For businesses and frequent transactions
Fixed Deposit (FD) Lock your money for a fixed time and earn higher interest
Recurring Deposit (RD) Deposit a fixed amount every month and earn interest

Debit vs Credit Cards

  • 💳 Debit Card: Spends your own money from your bank account
  • 💳 Credit Card: Allows you to borrow money and pay later

Why is Banking Important?

  • 🔒 Keeps your money safe
  • 🧾 Helps build your credit history
  • 💼 Allows easy access to loans and payments
  • 📈 Supports economic growth
