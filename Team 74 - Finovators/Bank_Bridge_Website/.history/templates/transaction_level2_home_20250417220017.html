🍬 Candy Coin Game

🎮 How to Play

Welcome to the Candy Coin Game! You are a candy shopper with limited coins.

🎯 Goal: Collect the maximum number of candies such that no coins are left

💡 You can Undo your last purchase using the Undo button

❗ You can only buy a candy if you have enough coins — no partial purchases!

🧩 Scenario 1: Basic All-or-Nothing Buying

🏁 You start with ₹20 coins.

🎯 Buy as many candies as you can using your coins.

💥 If you run out of coins or make a mistake, use Undo to retry!

Start Scenario 1

🧩 Scenario 2: Smart Buying Challenge

🏁 You have ₹18 coins and you already bought 1 Jelly Beans worth ₹12.

🎯 Goal: Collect exactly 2 chocolates, and the total number of candies should be 4.

🧠 Plan your buys smartly — meet the goal to win!
