Level 0: What is Transaction Management?

💰 What is Transaction Management?

Imagine you’re buying candy from a vending machine 🍬.
You press a button, put your money in, and wait for the candy to come out.
Now imagine the machine takes your money but gives no candy — that's not fair, right?

So, banks and computers use transaction management to make sure things like that don’t happen with your money.

🧠 How it Works (Simple Rules):

All or Nothing!
A transaction must finish completely or not at all.
Just like: You get candy or you get your money back — no cheating!

Safe and Secure
If something goes wrong (like a power cut ⚡), the bank cancels the transaction and keeps your money safe.

No Mix-ups
Even if 100 people are using the bank at once, the system keeps everyone's transaction separate and clean. Like separate lines in a candy shop!

No Negative Money!
You can’t send more money than you have.
The bank checks your balance first — just like you can’t buy candy if you don’t have enough coins!
